Wanderful

“Wanderful is a website that simplifies the rental experience for newcomers, by integrating searching, contacting, roommate finding, and appointment making into a straightforward process."

1. Problem Finding

“Finding a place to live is usually frustrating for a newcomer”

Tedious Information

Nonresponse Appointment

Unclear Location

“Renting process becomes even more complicated for them”

Time Limit

Money Limit

Roommate Matters

2. Problem Analysis

“Based on our research, three to four apps are usually used during the renting process (for source finding, communicating, navigating, etc.), along with manually filtering the features they care.”

3. Competitor Analysis

User friendly interface, have roommate finding function, live chat to poster/roommates; no corperation with property management company may limit their sources, not well-known, no introduction for surrounding environment(safety, nearby, etc.)

Good neighborhood guide map, detailed surrounding information, only focus on the information providing, not on the rental process and roommate finding process

Well-known, abundant sources; sources not up to date, extra fees charged for real estate posters.

Well-known, abundant sources; information not up to date, low response, no communication or management, no tracing for users.

Easy to post a source, live chat to poster/roommates; hard to manage historical sources, detailed information is usually not attached, no guaranty for the reliability.

Easy to chat and post; culture specific software; hard to manage historical sources, detailed information is usually not attached, no guaranty for the reliability.


“How about we could simplify this process within one app?”

See Wanderful


4. Design Solution

"Conversational registering process helps users establish preferences for property and roommates”

"Tag filters helps users filtering sources quickly”

"Interactive map gives let users be familiar with different districts”

"Easy Live chat enables users talk to poster/roommates immediate when interested in one property”

"Compare function helps users make their final decisions”

"Appointment manage function gives user a way to manage the routine of house showing”

"Personal page gives user a place to edit their profile, view their favorite sources, and manage their chatting history and appointments"
